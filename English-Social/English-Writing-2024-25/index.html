Nico Bellantoni's 8th Grade Writing

Welcome to my portfolio! Here you will find my writing from 8th grade English and social studies. This year I have three writing goals:

  1. I aim to use fewer commas to reduce comma-related grammar errors.
  2. I plan to make fewer silly grammatical errors while writing more quickly.
  3. I want to pursue English more seriously as a subject.
Inside Out & Back Again and Refugee Paragraphs

In this assignment to write the paragraphs on the effects of war in Inside Out & Back Again by Thanhha Lai and the brave actions of the main characters in Refugee by Alan Gratz, I learned about (enjoyed) the profound ways that conflict and hardship shape the resilience and character of young people like Ha, Isabel, Mahmoud, and Josef. For my writing skills, I was able to (learned how to) easily make grammatical corrections, however, I must still control my overusage of commas.
