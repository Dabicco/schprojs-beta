Nico Bellantoni's 8th Grade Writing

Welcome to my portfolio! Here you will find my writing from 8th grade English and social studies. This year I have three writing goals:

  1. I aim to use fewer commas to reduce comma-related grammar errors.
  2. I plan to make fewer silly grammatical errors while writing more quickly.
  3. I want to pursue English more seriously as I aspire to be an English teacher.

Inside Out & Back Again and Refugee Paragraphs

In this assignment to write the paragraphs on the effects of war in Inside Out & Back Again by Thanhha Lai and the brave actions of the main characters in Refugee by Alan Gratz, I learned about the profound ways that conflict and hardship shape the resilience and character of young people like Ha, Isabel, Mahmoud, and Josef. I also realized how important it is to care about others and their struggles, especially when they face tough times. For my writing skills, I was able to easily make grammatical corrections; however, I must still control my overusage of commas. I also learned the importance of empathy and understanding different perspectives.

Argument Paragraph Response

In social studies class, we wrote an argument essay on Reconstruction where I learned about the experiences of African Americans during the era of Reconstruction. I found it interesting how people from the south could be so arrogant when it came to the color of their skin. In writing, I focused more on controlling my usage of commas while writing, which is a problem I have improved upon in this piece. However, I need to work on my tense, being sure to keep a steady past, present, or future tense throughout the entire paragraph.

Title Analysis Essay

In this assignment to compare the themes of the novel Inside Out & Back Again by Thanhha Lai to the title, I learned how everything in the story, including the title, somehow clicks together in a compelling way. For my writing skills, I attempted to incorporate some of the teacher examples while still maintaining originality and fluency. This approach did occasionally confuse me, but overall I believe it worked out well. However, I still need to improve my organization, as in the second half of the essay I seem to have mixed up the examples and didn’t put the right one in, so I must be sure to look out for that.
